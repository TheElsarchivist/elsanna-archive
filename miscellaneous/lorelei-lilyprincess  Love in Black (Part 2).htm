Love in Black (Part 2)

Story by Nuffy
Illustration by LORELEI-LilyPrincess

*****

Golden light of dawn shone into the wide opened door, slightly warmed Anna’s frozen body. Unbelievably, she could feel the warm once again, but still chained with heavy solid ice around her wrists. Like woke up from a long deep slumber, the princess blinked several times, trying to focus at person before her. There, she met Queen Elsa, smiling warmly at her.

“Oh, you’re awake.”

“E…Elsa… P… please… l…let me go…”

“Hm? Oh Anna, my SWEET Anna, how can I let you go when I love you so, especially when you begging me with your sweet voice like that.” Elsa giggled.

“Do you really… love me?”

“Of course darling, I can do anything for you.”

“So let me go, please, Elsa. I’m begging you, please” The queen approached her little sister, gazing into the turquoise eyes before wrapping her arms around Anna’s waist and hug her dearly.

“You belong to me, sister, you’re mine, and mine alone.” Anna stunned with her sister’s word, confusing of what was happening.

“I… I know.”

“Really?” Elsa asked with an eyebrow quirked. “So what about this?” The queen stepped aside letting her beloved sister see what’s behind her.

The princess’s eyes widened as soon as she saw Prince Hans of the Southern Isles, soaked with the blood on the ice floor, as well as Kristoff, the ice harvester, lying unconsciousness beside him.

“Hans! Kristoff!”

“I spared the ice harvester’s life because he had assisted you to get here. But for the Prince of the Southern Isles,” Elsa turned back to meet Anna, fire of hatred burning furiously in her beautiful icy blue eyes. “I will not condone man who is taking you away from me! Never!”

“What have you done, Elsa! You’re making a war!”

“For you to be MINE, I can and I will do anything.”

Tears ran down on Anna’s cheeks. Her entire body shivered with fear.

“I… I will stay, Elsa.”

“Really?” Elsa asked in disbelief, “Do you really mean that Anna?”

“Yes…” The princess replied “If only you thaw this eternal winter and restore Arendelle. I will be yours… forever.”

“And do whatever I want?” The queen asked.

“Everything and anything you desire, sister.”

Elsa smiled and thawed the entire curse over Arendell, then hugged her sister tightly.

“Anna, I long for you for all my life, and now you are mine,” Elsa pressed her lips on Anna’s forehead.

“Mine,” On her pink blossom cheek.

“Mine alone.” And on her soft shivered lips.

Anna, on the other hand, gathered all her strength, holding a piece of ice as sharp as knife, aimed at Elsa’s heart on the back.

“Yes, Elsa…”

Anna softly whispered in the queen’s ears,

"I’m all yours…"
